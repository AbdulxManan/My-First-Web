Frequently Asked Questions

- What is Czech Virtual Agent?

We’re a dedicated service that assists expats in Czechia and Slovakia by providing expert virtual assistant support. We help you manage tasks, overcome language barriers, find doctors, book appointments, and handle daily life administration with ease.

- How does it work?

Simply reach out to us via our website or contact channels, and our virtual assistants will get back to you in 24 hours. Whether it's scheduling appointments, locating local professionals, or managing daily tasks, we’ll tell you the cost and timeframe and handle it all for you.

- What tasks can Czech Virtual Agent help with?

Our virtual assistants can help with various tasks, including but not limited to finding doctors, booking medical appointments, locating local services and professionals, handling administrative tasks, and providing valuable insights into life in Czechia and Slovakia.

- Are there any upfront fees or hidden costs?

We maintain transparent pricing with no hidden costs. Our service fees are communicated upfront, and you'll only be charged for the specific assistance you request.

- How can I become a Virtual Agent?

If you have a passion for helping others and possess excellent communication skills in English and Czech/Slovak, you could be a perfect fit for our team! Simply reach out to us in our contact form and we will get back to you with more details.
